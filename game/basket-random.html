Character Movement

  • Move Left and Right: Use the A (left) and D (right) keys on the keyboard to move your character across the court.
  • Jump: Press the W key to make your character jump. This is important for blocking shots and shooting the ball.

Defense

  • Blocking Shots: Use your character's jump ability to block or intercept the ball when the opponent attempts to shoot. Timing your jump correctly can help prevent the ball from going into your hoop.
  • Steal the Ball: You can also try to get the ball from the opponent by jumping in front of them and intercepting the ball during their attempts to shoot or move.

Attack

  • Shoot the Ball: Press the Spacebar to throw the ball toward the hoop. Try to aim the shot while controlling the character's position.
  • Positioning: Make sure your character is properly aligned with the hoop. Move quickly to get into a good shooting position when you have the ball.
