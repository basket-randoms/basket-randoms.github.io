How to Play Basketball Stars on All Devices

How to Play Description:
Learn the controls and master Basketball Stars across all your devices.


🖥️ Desktop Controls

Action Control
Action ⌨️ Player 1
Move ⌨️ A / D
Jump ⌨️ W
Shoot / Steal ⌨️ B
Special Shot ⌨️ V

🎯 Desktop Tip: Steal the ball when your opponent is about to shoot for an easy advantage.

📱 Mobile Controls

Action Control
Move 👆 Virtual Joystick
Jump 👆 Tap Jump Icon
Shoot / Steal 👆 Tap Shoot Button
Special Shot 👆 Tap Skill Button

🎯 Mobile Tip: Special shots are powerful but need perfect timing.

📱 Tablet Controls

Action Control
Move 👆 On-Screen Arrows
Jump 👆 Tap Jump Button
Shoot / Steal 👆 Tap Action Button
Special Shot 👆 Tap Power Icon

🎯 Tablet Tip: Tablets are great for 2-player matches thanks to their bigger screen.
