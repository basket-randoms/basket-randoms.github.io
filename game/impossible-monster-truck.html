How to Play Impossible Monster Truck on All Devices

How to Play Description:


🖥️ Desktop Controls

Action Control
Accelerate ⌨️ W / ⬆ Arrow
Brake ⌨️ S / ⬇ Arrow
Steer Left ⌨️ A / ⬅ Arrow
Steer Right ⌨️ D / ➡ Arrow

🎯 Desktop Tip: Don’t oversteer — balance is the key to survival.

📱 Mobile Controls

Action Control
Accelerate 👆 Tap right pedal
Brake 👆 Tap left pedal
Steer 👆 ⬅➡ Tilt device

🎯 Mobile Tip: Tilt smoothly to avoid falling from tracks.

📱 Tablet Controls

Action Control
Accelerate 👆 Touch pedal
Brake 👆 Touch brake
Steer 👆 ⬅➡ Tilt tablet

🎯 Tablet Tip: Play on bigger screens for easier navigation of thin tracks.
