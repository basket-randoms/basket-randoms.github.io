What Is An Idle Game?

Idle Games (sometimes called clicker games or incremental games) are a genre where progress happens automatically over time, even if you're not actively playing. Players usually start by performing simple tasks, like clicking to earn money or mining resources, and then use those rewards to buy upgrades that accelerate progress.


Gamers love idle gameplay because:

  • It's relaxing and requires minimal effort.
  • You can multitask while still making progress.
  • The upgrade system makes it addictive as you grow faster with each session.

Idle Games are popular among students, office workers, and casual players who enjoy progress-based fun without stress.
